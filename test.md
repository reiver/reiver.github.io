# This Is A tEsT!

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux

A man befriends a bear, who acts as his protector.
One day, while the man sleeps, the bear tries to kill a fly on the man's face with a stone.
The bear kills the fly but also kills his friend.

This illustrates that an unwise friend can be more dangerous than an enemy.
