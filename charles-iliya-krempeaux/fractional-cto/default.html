Summary

Charles Iliya Krempeaux is a technology executive with more-than 30 years of experience in software engineering, product development, and engineering leadership. He has a proven track record of building and scaling engineering teams, shipping products from zero to market, and guiding start-ups through critical growth stages.

Charles is an engineering leader, product leader, and entrepreneur who has been a foundational figure in several prominent tech companies and startups, and is known for building core Internet and financial technologies.

Charles is also the founding CTO of Koho — the first neo-bank in Canada.

He is available as a fractional CTO to help early-stage start-ups with rapid prototyping, building MVPs from the ground up, crafting investor pitch-decks, pitching to investors, recruiting talent, defining technical strategy, cultivating engineering culture, and driving results.

And, he is available as a fractional CTO to help growth-stage companies with refactoring MVPs and legacy architectures to scalable, distributed, secure, and resilient enterprise-grade architetures, managing technical debt by refactoring and replacing quick-and-dirty early code with stable infrastructure, implementing SOC 2 compliance, implementing uptime strategies, disaster recovery, and high-availability systems, optimizing cloud infrastructure spending, structuring expanding engineering & product orgs, and driving measurable results.

Alumni of: Electronic Arts (EA), Hootsuite, Koho Financial, SpaceHost, Trulioo.
