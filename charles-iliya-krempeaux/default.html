Charles Iliya Krempeaux

Charles Ⅲ

Charles Iliya Krempeaux is the eldest son of Charles Robert Krempeaux (i.e., his father, who usually went by the name “Charlie”, but also sometimes went by “Chaz”) & Malekeh Agha Safavi (i.e., his mother).

Charles Iliya Krempeaux is married to Hyerim Kang (who goes by “Bella”, which is the English translation of the Korean name “Hyerim”). They have 2 children — an older son, and a younger daughter.
