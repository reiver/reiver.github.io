Charles Iliya Krempeaux's family-name — “Krempeaux” — has a Polish Ashkenazi origin. The Polish Ashkenazi family name Krępowiecki was anglicized as Krempowiecki — where the Polish letter “ę” became “em” in English. In England, Krempowiecki was then shortened to Krempo. And later Krempo was given a French spelling to become Krempeaux.
